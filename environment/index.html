Earth is in its 6th mass extinction event. One million species are threatened with extinction. Deforestation, overfishing, and habitat destruction are the primary drivers.

We are losing biodiversity at 100 to 1,000 times the natural background rate — and with it, the ecosystem services that human civilization depends on.

What Actually Works 30x30 Initiative

The global commitment to protect 30% of land and ocean by 2030. If achieved, this would preserve critical habitats and create corridors for species migration. Agreed to at COP15 by 196 countries.
Payments for Ecosystem Services

PES programs pay landowners and communities to preserve forests, wetlands, and other ecosystems. Costa Rica's program has been a model — the country doubled its forest cover.
Sustainable Agriculture

Regenerative farming practices can produce food while restoring soil health and reducing habitat destruction. The transition requires policy support and farmer education.
Marine Protected Areas

Science-based fishing quotas and marine protected areas allow fish populations and ocean ecosystems to recover. Where implemented, they work remarkably well.
Reduce Meat Consumption

Beef production is the single largest driver of deforestation. Reducing beef consumption — even modestly — has outsized environmental benefits.
How to Actually Do It
  1. Support 30x30 legislation at national and local levels
  2. Reduce beef consumption — even modest changes help
  3. Fund land trusts and conservation easements
  4. Support sustainable fishing certifications (MSC)
  5. Advocate for regenerative agriculture subsidies
 What Digital Makers Can Do

You design and build digital things. Here's how those skills apply.

 Individual

Choose sustainable hosting providers. Advocate for performance budgets on your projects — smaller, faster sites use less energy. Use your design skills to make conservation compelling, not guilt-inducing.

 Community

Build digital tools for local conservation groups — species tracking apps, volunteer coordination platforms, land trust donation flows. Help community gardens and urban farms establish an online presence.

 Country

Create open-source tools for monitoring deforestation, water quality, and biodiversity. Build platforms that connect citizen scientists with researchers. Design interfaces for environmental impact data that hold corporations and governments accountable.

 World

Contribute to platforms like Global Forest Watch and iNaturalist. Build accessible, multilingual interfaces for marine protected area monitoring. Design supply chain transparency tools that help consumers understand the ecological cost of what they buy.
