1 in 8 people globally live with a mental health disorder. Depression and anxiety are leading causes of disability worldwide. Suicide kills 700,000 people every year.

Despite this enormous burden, mental health receives a fraction of the funding and attention given to physical health conditions.

What Actually Works Integrate into Primary Care

The treatment gap for mental health is enormous. Integrating mental health screening and basic treatment into primary care is the most scalable way to close it.
Task-Sharing Models

Training community health workers to deliver basic mental health support extends access far beyond what specialist systems can provide. WHO's mhGAP program has proven this works.
Reduce Stigma

Public awareness campaigns that normalize mental health help-seeking have measurable effects on treatment rates. Countries that have invested in stigma reduction see better outcomes.
Digital Mental Health Tools

App-based and telehealth mental health tools are expanding access, particularly in underserved areas. The evidence base is growing and some tools show outcomes comparable to in-person therapy.
Address Root Causes

Poverty, violence, social isolation, and precarity are root causes of mental health crises. Addressing upstream determinants prevents downstream suffering.
How to Actually Do It
  1. Advocate for mental health parity in insurance coverage
  2. Fund WHO mhGAP and similar global programs
  3. Support local crisis hotlines and peer support networks
  4. Push for mental health education in schools
  5. Address social determinants — housing, employment, community
 What Digital Makers Can Do

You design and build digital things. Here's how those skills apply.

 Individual

Design humane digital experiences. Question dark patterns, infinite scroll, notification bombardment, and engagement metrics that treat human attention as a resource to extract. Build things that respect people's time and mental state.

 Community

Build accessible websites for local crisis centers and mental health organizations. Create tools that connect people to peer support networks. Design onboarding flows for therapy platforms that reduce the friction of asking for help.

 Country

Build platforms that make mental health resources findable and navigable — therapist directories, insurance coverage tools, crisis line integrations. Design public awareness campaigns that normalize help-seeking.

 World

Contribute to digital mental health tools designed for low-resource settings. Build crisis intervention platforms that work across languages and cultures. Design AI-assisted screening tools that extend the reach of limited mental health workforces — with appropriate human oversight and ethical guardrails.
